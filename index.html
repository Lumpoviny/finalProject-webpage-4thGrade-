Hello.

Welcome to this webpage. It's my personal blog where I will try to show you my life, friends, experiences and stuff. I don't know what all should be on this page, but I made it the way I felt it should be. Well...see for yourself.

In the next 3 paragraphs I've decided to tell you what do I value about the relationships among me and my friends, and things it brings to my life.

Advices

Friends can help you a lot. Whether it's by knowing them and asking yourself that if he can do it, why couldn't I. But also if you don't know the way forward, it's great to have someone you can turn to.

Pushing one another

It's always good to have a reason to try that much harder to achieve a goal. In my opinion, having a friend who is better than you in certain areas is a great gift because it motivates you to improve yourself so that you don't fall behind.

Not feeling alone

The worst feeling is when you feel like nobody cares about you and you don't mean anything. We all experience this from time to time, but if you have good friends, these states go away much faster. You know that someone cares about you.
